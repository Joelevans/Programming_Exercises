Used for values of type float, double and decimal.

They use the following indications:
'f' and 'F' as suffixes : float.
'd' and 'D' as suffixes : double.
'm' and 'M' as suffixes : decimal
'e' is an exponent, eg "e-5" means the integer part 
multiplied by 10^-5.
